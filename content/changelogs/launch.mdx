BetterReply is an AI-driven platform designed to enhance customer interactions and streamline communication through automated responses.

### Features

- **Trustworthy, Accurate Answers**: With features like "Confidence score," ensure your chatbot provides reliable responses.
- **Lead Generation Engine**: Collect leads and gather customer data while delivering a personalized experience.
- **Automated Customer Support**: Let the AI handle customer queries, improving response times and customer satisfaction.
- **Advanced Analytics**: Gain insights into chatbot interactions to continuously improve performance and user experience.
- **Seamless Email Integration**: Connect your email account for direct AI responses to customer inquiries.
