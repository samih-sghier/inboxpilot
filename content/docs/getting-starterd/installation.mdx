## Installation and Setup

### Getting Started

Welcome to bettereply! Follow these steps to set up your dashboard and start managing your email interactions effectively.

### 1. Create an Account

1. Visit the [bettereply Sign-Up Page](https://bettereply.com/signup).
2. Enter your email address, create a password, and fill out any other required fields.
3. Click **Sign Up** to create your account.
4. Check your email for a verification link and follow the instructions to verify your email address.

### 2. Log In

1. Visit the [bettereply Login Page](https://bettereply.com/login).
2. Enter your email address and password.
3. Click **Log In** to access your dashboard.

### 3. Create an Organization

1. Once logged in, go to the **Organizations** tab in the sidebar.
2. Click the **Create New Organization** button.
3. Enter your organization name, contact information, and any other required details.
4. Click **Create Organization** to save your new organization.

### 4. Add Your Sources

1. Navigate to the **Sources** tab on your dashboard.
2. Click the **Add Source** button.
3. Provide the necessary details for your data sources, such as URLs or file uploads.
4. Click **Save Source** to add it to your list.

### 5. Provide Sample Emails

1. Go to the **Sample Emails** section within your dashboard.
2. Click the **Add Sample Email** button.
3. Fill in the brief description and the email content.
4. Click **Save Sample Email** to store it for training your AI.

### 6. Train Your AI

1. Navigate to the **Training** section on your dashboard.
2. Select the sources and sample emails you want to use for training.
3. Click the **Train AI** button to start the training process.
4. Monitor the training progress and ensure that the AI is learning from the provided data.

### 7. Connect Your Email Account

1. Go to the **Connect** tab in your dashboard.
2. Click the **Connect Email** button.
3. Follow the prompts to link your email account to bettereply.
4. Ensure that the connection is successful and that the AI can access your email for responding to queries.

### 8. Set Up Escalation Handling

1. Navigate to the **Escalations** section in your dashboard.
2. Define the criteria for when an email should be escalated (e.g., specific keywords or unanswered queries).
3. Set up notifications for escalated emails to ensure timely responses.
4. Click **Save Settings** to apply your escalation preferences.

### 9. Explore Your Dashboard

- Customize your dashboard by adding or removing widgets to suit your needs.
- Use the various features to monitor feedback, manage notifications, and stay updated with alerts.

### Need Help?

For assistance with API and CRM integrations, or if you have any questions, please reach out to us at [support@bettereply.com](mailto:support@bettereply.com).
