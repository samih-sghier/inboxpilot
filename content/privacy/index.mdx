# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: October 16, 2024**

At Inboxpilot, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of your data when you interact with our platform. ## How We Collect and Use Your Personal Information

Inboxpilot does not store your emails, data, or personal information. We only store the responses generated by our AI, and these responses are encrypted to ensure that no human can read your emails. 

We may collect the following information necessary to provide our AI-driven services:

- **User Information**: Name, email address, and any other details necessary for account setup.
- **Interaction Data**: Data related to your interactions with our AI, including queries made and responses received.

We do not sell personal information to anyone and only share it with third parties who facilitate the delivery of our services.

## Use of the Inboxpilot Website

As is true of most websites, Inboxpilot’s website collects certain information automatically and stores it in log files. This information may include:

- Internet Protocol (IP) addresses
- Browser type
- Operating system
- Usage information about the use of Inboxpilot’s website, including a history of the pages you view

We use this information to help us design our site to better suit our users’ needs and to diagnose problems with our server.

## Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Inboxpilot uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience on our website. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. For more information, please refer to our comprehensive Cookie Notice.

## Sharing Information with Third Parties

The personal information we collect from you is stored in databases hosted by third parties located in the United States. These third parties do not use or have access to your personal information for any purpose other than cloud storage and retrieval. We may engage third parties to send information to you, including information about our products, services, and events.

We do not otherwise reveal your personal data to non-Inboxpilot persons or businesses for their independent use unless:

1. You request or authorize it.
2. It’s in connection with Inboxpilot-hosted events.
3. The information is provided to comply with the law.
4. The information is provided to our agents, vendors, or service providers who perform functions on our behalf.
5. To address emergencies or legal disputes.

## Data Transfer and Security

Information we collect about you will be processed in the United States. By using Inboxpilot’s services, you acknowledge that your personal information will be processed in the United States. We implement robust data protection mechanisms, including:

- **Encryption**: All customer data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption algorithms both at rest and in transit.
- **Access Control**: We employ user roles and permissions to ensure that only authorized personnel can access sensitive data.

## Your Rights

You have the following rights concerning the information we hold:

- **Right to Access**: Request access to the information we have on record.
- **Right to Rectification**: Request corrections to any inaccurate information.
- **Right to Erasure**: Request the removal of your information from our records, subject to legal and operational constraints.

If you wish to confirm that Inboxpilot is processing your personal data or to have access to the personal data we may have about you, please contact us.

## Children’s Data

We do not knowingly attempt to solicit or receive information from children.
